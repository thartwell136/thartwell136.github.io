Round 2: Design Duel

Which processor-based laptop provides more design choices and operating system options to users?

Desgin & OS Options

M3 Max MacBook Pro Laptops offer:

MacOS as the only OS choice

No touchscreen

Only clamshell mode

Minimal ports

13th Gen Intel® Core™ i9 mobile processor-based laptops offer:

Multiple OS choices such as Windows, Chrome, Linux, and more

Touchscreen (even dual-touch screens)

Clamshell, tent, tablet modes

Detachable and convertible form factors

More port options

Defeating the competition with ease, 13th Gen Intel® Core™ i9 mobile processor-based laptops enable customers to choose their design and OS, whereas with Apple, they have no options to choose from.

Round 3: Compatibility Quest

Which processor will knock out the competition with post-purchase upgrade options and multi-device support?

M3 Max MacBook Pro Laptops have:

No post-purchase upgrades

Exclusive compatibility with other Apple products– such as iOS, iPadOS, and macOS only

13th Gen Intel® Core™ i9 mobile processor-based laptops have:

Customizability options pre- purchase and post-purchase

Intel® Unison™ software, which works across OSs to integrate Android or iOS-based phones and tablets with a user’s PC

Crushing the competition, 13th Gen Intel® Core™ i9 mobile processor-based laptops offer customization options and increased device compatibility with Intel® Unison™ software.

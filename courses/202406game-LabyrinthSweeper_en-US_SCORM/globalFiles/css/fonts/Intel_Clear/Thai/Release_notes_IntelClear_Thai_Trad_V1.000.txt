==Release notes_Intel Clear Thai Trad==

Date of creation: 2016-02-24

Changes since previous release:
- This is the first release.

Basic information
1 Basic family name: Intel Clear Thai Trad
2 TrueType Version record: 1.000
3 Supported scripts: Latin, Thai
4 Number of glyphs: 389
5 Weights/Styles: Light, Regular, Bold
6 Usage: Text
7 
Non-Latin characters sets, except Chinese, include a basic set of Latin characters as standard.  Korean, Thai Traditional, Thai Modern, Devanagari, Hebrew, Arabic and Japanese are missing the following glyphs shown below; they can be accessed by downloading the Latin font set.

Technology
1 Files formats: The files have been delivered in the TrueType-based OpenType format, for use in all major Operating Systems.
2 Embedding settings: Everything is allowed (installable mode); subsetting is allowed.
3 Hinting information: All glyphs are manually hinted for optimized rasterization in Microsoft ClearType or for other LCD devices.
